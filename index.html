Who Are We?

At CarpeDiem, we believe in making learning and creativity accessible and fun. Established in 2024, our team of innovators, educators, and gamers are passionate about pushing boundaries.

We blend technology with imagination to develop tools and content that genuinely impact students, creators, and curious minds alike. Our mission is to transform conventional learning and entertainment into engaging, memorable adventures.

For general ease of use, we have three seperate groups/teams. CarpeDiem Studios, CarpeDiem Notes, and CarpeDiem AI. These are usually shortened to CDS, CDN, and CDAI (respectively).
